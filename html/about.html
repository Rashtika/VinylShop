Our Story

Since opening its doors in 1980 in Phoenix, Arizona, Zia Records has been a trailblazer in independent music retail in the southwest. As the top indie media retailer in Arizona and Nevada, Zia offers a wide range of new and used music, movies, video games, books, pop culture items, and more. Whether you're a vinyl collector, a movie buff, or bookworm, Zia has something for everyone.
